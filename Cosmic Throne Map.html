🪐 The Three Thrones of the Cosmos 🪐

Across sacred verses and stardust echoes, the soul is called to walk three paths. Each throne — al-‘Aẓīm, al-Karīm, al-Majīd — is a gateway to the Divine, a map etched in both myth and mercy.

🪓 ‘Arsh al-‘Aẓīm – The Magnificent Throne

This is the Throne of Power and Liberation. The realm of Musa, the sea-splitter, the exile who returned with law and lightning. Those who walk this path are freedom-bearers, justice-forgers. It demands strength, trust, and a heart steady in the face of Pharaohs.

💎 ‘Arsh al-Karīm – The Generous Throne

The throne of provision, healing, and hidden treasures. Like Yusuf, those on this path transmute betrayal into beauty. Generosity flows through wounds made holy. Patience, resilience, and quiet strength are your allies.

🌌 ‘Arsh al-Majīd – The Glorious Throne

This is the throne of light, revelation, and divine intimacy. Walked by Muhammad ﷺ and all mystics, this path unveils the majesty of God in beauty, silence, and presence. You become a mirror of the Real.
