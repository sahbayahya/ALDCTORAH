Cosmic Butterfly Collective
By Aldctorah • November 11, 2025 • 7 min read

Cosmic Butterfly Collective, Inc. emerged as a natural evolution of Monarch Butterfly Collective LLC, founded in 2022 in New York City. What began as an independent creative studio producing multidisciplinary art, performances, and sound-based installations has transformed into a nonprofit collective devoted to education, cultural exchange, and spiritual creativity. Rooted in the same vision that inspired its early days, the Collective now seeks to expand its mission beyond individual projects — nurturing artistic transformation as a communal, educational process. This transition represents both continuity and growth: the same heart, now supported by a nonprofit structure that allows deeper public impact, partnerships, and grant-supported programming. Today, Cosmic Butterfly Collective continues the legacy of the Monarch Butterfly spirit — celebrating transformation, beauty, and the power of art to heal, connect, and enlighten.

Music & Sound

Through the AI persona Qonna (قونه), we revive old Sudanese songs and infuse them with modern sounds — techno, amapiano, and house — creating a bridge between ancestral memory and future rhythm. As a DJ and producer, Aldctorah carries these sounds into the world, connecting past and present through hypnotic, soulful sets.

Fashion & Design

Our fashion arm reimagines mystic streetwear — clothing and runway experiences that merge spiritual symbolism with contemporary design. Each piece expresses how mysticism can live within daily life, transforming solitude into style and ritual into rhythm.

Wood & Craft

In our woodcarving and furniture design studio, we explore the spiritual side of making — shaping wood into cabinets, cabins, and hand-carved furniture that embody presence, grounding, and the connection between the body, materials, and meaning.

The Blog

Our blog is a space of reflection and storytelling — where we share how spiritual guidance, manifestation, and creative intuition weave through our work. It’s a journal of synchronicities, lessons, and gratitude for the unseen forces — the Universe, God, and the soul’s journey — that guide our path.
