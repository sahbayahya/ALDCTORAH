Meet QONNA (قونه)

<<<<<<< HEAD

In Sudanese neighborhoods, “Qonna” is the name of a woman who accepts her voice to be the voice of joy in the neighborhood. Crowned by the spirit of joy, she preaches freedom through sound, no celebration without her, and the world smiles and because she exists. Together, Qonna and ALDCTORAH bridge the old and the unseen, bringing ancestral frequencies into today’s pulse. Each track becomes a prayer in motion — a dialogue between human hands and digital heartbeats. “Qonna sings to remind us that nothing sacred is ever lost.”
