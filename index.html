Manifest & Method

We work intuitively — sketching, sampling, carving the dream — allowing it to manifest through the chosen medium. The process still astonishes us, mysterious and far from understood, yet we trust the method completely, for there is nothing in this world more beautiful than the movement of this butterfly. Our pieces are limited, intentional, and made with reverence. Every release and object is an invitation to slow down and remember.
