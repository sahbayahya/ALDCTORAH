What We Build

Cosmic Butterfly Collective is a multidisciplinary creative studio working across music (Qonna), fashion (SHEMMA), and experiential hospitality (Monarch Café). We combine artistic vision with disciplined process to develop cohesive identities, immersive experiences, and culturally rooted narratives. Our approach is intentional and patient — allowing concepts to mature until the full picture is clear. Each project is designed to create lasting impact: music as experience, garments as identity, and environments that foster connection and memory. Interested in collaborations, custom designs, experiential projects, or private events? Contact us.

Manifest & Method

We work intuitively — sketching, sampling, carving the dream — allowing it to manifest through the chosen medium. The process still astonishes us, mysterious and far from understood, yet we trust the method completely, for there is nothing in this world more beautiful than the movement of this butterfly. Our pieces are limited, intentional, and made with reverence. Every release and object is an invitation to slow down and remember.
